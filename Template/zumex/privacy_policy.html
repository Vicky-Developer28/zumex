Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Zumex, we may collect information from users only when it is necessary to provide our services. These services include website development, poster design, and social media management.

Depending on the project, we may request information such as your name, contact details, brand name, logo, business information, social media details, content preferences, and other relevant materials. We collect this information only with your permission and only for the purpose of delivering the requested service.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to complete the services you request from Zumex. For example:

  • For website development: we may use your brand name, logo, business details, and content.
  • For poster design: we may use your branding materials, design requirements, and promotional information.
  • For social media management: we may use information related to your business, audience, content strategy, and social media accounts.

Your information is used only to help us provide high-quality services and improve your experience with Zumex.

3. Sharing of Information

Zumex respects your privacy. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or business information with third parties. Any information you provide remains confidential and is used only by Zumex for the purpose of delivering the requested service. We take privacy seriously and are committed to protecting the information entrusted to us.

4. How We Protect Your Information

At Zumex, we take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us. All client information, project materials, and business details are handled with care and stored securely.

Access to your information is limited only to authorized members of the Zumex team who require it to provide the requested services. We do not disclose, misuse, or distribute your information without your permission unless required by law.

While we strive to maintain the highest level of security, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure. However, we continuously work to safeguard your information and maintain its confidentiality.

5. Your Rights Over Your Data

As a user of Zumex, you have the right to know what information we collect and how it is used.

  • You may request access to the information you have provided to us.
  • You may ask for corrections to inaccurate information.
  • You may request the removal of your information from our records, subject to any legal or contractual obligations.
  • You may withdraw your consent for us to use your information at any time.

Upon receiving such a request, we will take reasonable steps to update, delete, or restrict the use of your information where applicable. If you have any questions regarding your data or privacy rights, you may contact Zumex directly for assistance.
